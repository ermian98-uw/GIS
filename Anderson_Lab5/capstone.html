Instances where the entire state of Washington does NOT contain temperature data indicate there were no cloudless ECOSTRESS flybys during the represented timeframe. This may seem strange, but during the morning hours and colder months, clouds are more prolific in the Pacific Northwest, and the ECOSTRESS sensor passes overhead, at most, every 48 hours. This means it can easily miss a large swath of land, say, in the morning, week after week.

Capstone Proposal (in progress)

(Update 06/08/2021)

My expected paper title is: "Pinpointing land and surface air temperature variability across topographically diverse locales in Washington State via spatial joining of spaceborne and terrestrial data".

Essentially, my project examines the difference between surface temperature and air temperature accross Washington state over a one-year time period. Surface temperature is remotely sensed via the ECOSTRESS sensor (70x70 meter spatial resolution) aboard the International Space Station (about 1,300 valid flybys in the one-year period). Air temperature is compiled via a web harvester scanning over 5,000 active in-situ Weather Underground weather stations. Various data points, such as land cover type and sun elevation at time of weather observation, will contribute to the ulimate regression analysis I do to predict the values I need to "penalize" the surface temperature raster into getting air temperature across the entire state. I find it fascinating to investigate more granular temperature estimation/forecasting methods (anomalies could be a mere 100 meters apart and would otherwise be invisible on a wider map). A lot of research has already been done examining this "heat island" effect - my niche is trying to find miniature "heat islands" across ALL land terrains!

Here is a very very rough glimpse into the type of work I'm doing. The photo represents a kriging interpolation of the temperature "penalties" across all terrains averaged for the entire year. Pinker regions suggest surface temperature is much higher than air temperature, while green regions suggest the opposite. TBA!!!
